Tom Hardy

Edward Thomas Hardy is a British actor, born on September 15, 1977 in London. He is known for his roles in Inception, Warrior, The Dark Knight Rises, Mad Max: Fury Road, and Venom.Tom, an only child, was born in Hammersmith and grew up in East Sheen , London . His mother, Anne, was an Irish Catholic artist , and his father, Edward (Chips) Hardy, was a comedy writer. Hardy attended Reeds , Tower House and Richmond Theatre Schools before joining Drama Centre London (where he studied under the tutor who taught Anthony Hopkins ). He began his career in war dramas, playing US Army Private John Janovec in Band of Brothers (2001). He made his film debut in Ridley Scott's war thriller Black Hawk Down.
